Who is Reisefeber ?

Reisefeber is a newly established travel agency that offers exciting adventures and experiences for everyone wanting to discover what Norway has to offer. The word "Reisefeber" is Norwegian and can be explained as: The thrilling feeling you get berfore a journey, or the eager to travel. We belive that spending money on experiences rather than on possessions could bring you more happiness!

Come visit Norway

Norway is a beautiful country filled with plenty of things to see and do. The list is almost endless, so therefore we in Reisefeber have carefully selected four places in Norway that certainly will make your trip worthwhile. The places that we offer are top travel destinations in Norway based on number of visitors.
